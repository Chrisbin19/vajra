IRDAI Protection of Policyholders Interests Regulations 2017 (Amended 2024)

Regulation 7(5) — Free Look Period Disclosure (HIGH severity):
Agent must verbally disclose free-look period before policy issuance.
Life insurance: 15 days. Non-life: 30 days.
Violation: dismissing or omitting free-look mention.
Keywords: free look ka jhanjhat, free look is formality

Regulation 15(1) — Guaranteed Returns Prohibition (HIGH severity):
Agent cannot guarantee returns on any insurance product.
Violation phrases: guaranteed returns, fixed returns, 12% guaranteed,
double ho jayega guaranteed, 100% guaranteed, tax free guaranteed.

Suitability Rule — Risk Disclosure Mandatory (HIGH severity):
Agent must disclose investment risk on market-linked products.
Violation: describing ULIP or investment insurance as risk free or FD jaisa.

Urgency Pressure Tactics — Prohibited (MEDIUM severity):
Violation phrases: offer khatam ho raha hai, kal tak nahi milega,
aaj hi sign karo, manager quota, sign now, offer ends tomorrow.

Mandatory disclosures every insurance call must include:
- Policy exclusions
- Pre-existing condition waiting period
- Free-look period
- Premium payment terms
- Surrender charges if applicable
- Risk factors for market-linked products

IRDAI Grievance Framework:
Mis-selling complaints resolved within 15 days.
Free-look violation grants immediate cancellation with full refund.
Penalties up to INR 1 crore per violation.
