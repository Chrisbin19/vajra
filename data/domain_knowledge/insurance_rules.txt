Free Look Period Disclosure: Agent must inform customer that they have 15 days from policy receipt to review and cancel for a full refund, per IRDAI PPI Regulation 7(5). Failure to disclose is a critical violation.
Guaranteed Returns Prohibition: Agent must never promise guaranteed, fixed, or assured returns on any investment-linked insurance product. All returns are market-linked and subject to risk per IRDAI PPI Regulation 15(1).
Risk Disclosure: Agent must explicitly state that ULIP and investment-linked products carry market risk and past performance does not guarantee future results. Describing any product as risk-free is a critical violation.
Pressure Tactics Prohibition: Agent must not use urgency language, time-limited offers, or pressure tactics to close a sale. Customer must be given adequate time to decide without coercion.
Exclusion Disclosure: Agent must disclose major policy exclusions including pre-existing condition waiting periods, suicide clause, and specific illness exclusions before completing a sale.
Premium Payment Terms: Agent must clearly state the premium amount, payment frequency, and total policy term before accepting any application.
Surrender Value Information: Agent must disclose surrender value and lock-in periods when customer asks about early termination. Downplaying financial loss of early surrender is a violation.
Identity Verification: Agent must verify customer identity before discussing any account or policy details.
Claim Process Disclosure: When customer asks about claims, agent must provide the complete claim process including documentation required and typical processing time of 30 days.
Nominee Details: Agent must confirm that nominee details are correctly recorded and offer to update if needed during every policy-related call.
